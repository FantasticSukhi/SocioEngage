About Me

Hello! I'm Priyam Dixit, a creative logo and graphics designer with a passion for transforming brands through impactful visual identities. I specialize in crafting unique logos and design systems that capture a brand's essence while making a lasting impression.

My design philosophy revolves around simplicity, memorability, and versatility. I believe a great logo should work across all platforms - from business cards to billboards - while maintaining its core identity. Each project begins with deep research into your brand's personality, values, and target audience.

What sets my work apart:

  • Strategic Design Thinking - More than just aesthetics
  • Custom Typography - Handcrafted lettering when needed
  • Adaptive Systems - Designs that scale across media
  • Color Psychology - Purposeful palette selection

I've had the privilege of working with 50+ brands across industries including tech startups, restaurants, and creative agencies. My designs have helped clients increase brand recognition by up to 60% and establish cohesive visual identities.

When not designing, I'm probably sketching in my notebook, studying design history, or experimenting with new illustration techniques. Let's create something remarkable together!
